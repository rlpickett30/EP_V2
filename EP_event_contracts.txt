MASTER EVENT PUBLISHER LIST
EnviroPulse V2
Status: Draft from drawings, not code-verified

Rule:
“Publisher” means the subsystem that places the event onto that system’s local event bus.
For inbound network events, the original source may be another system, but the local publisher is the Communication-listener.
